Yung Kee greatly honoured to present essence of Hong Kong’s Cantonese cuisine before President Hu Jintao

Industry’s Highest Tribute Adds Colour to
Yung Kee’s 65th Anniversary Commemoration

On 17 March 2007, Mr Kinsen Kam, Yung Kee Restaurant Group’s Director & General Manager, received a special tribute at the ‘King of Catering Award 2007” for his valuable contribution made to Hong Kong’s catering industry.

The honour came as a timely celebration of the restaurant’s 65th anniversary. Accepting the award, Mr Kam said, ‘not only does this award belong to Yung Kee’s remarkable traditions and team work, it also belongs to every member of the catering business. Over the years, we have been tirelessly promoting Hong Kong’s food culture to the world. I truly believe that with continued collaboration, we will achieve higher heights in enhancing our city’s reputation as the Gourmet Capital.’

The Award ceremony was broadcast on TVB Jade channel and will be re-broadcast to various TV channels in Mainland China.
